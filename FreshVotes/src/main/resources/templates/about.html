About FreshVotes Project
FreshVotes is a resume project created by Alex Kvasha. Idea was taken from the YouTuber Trevore Page but the whole web-app was written by me. This is the app where people can offer new features for products.
Imagine you have a product, for example some application. You want to make your application better but to make it better you have to know what to add or to update. This is the part where you need some reviews on your product but most of them are about nothing. To find really helpfull review isn't the easiest thing to do.
That is where FreshVotes come. It allows users to offer features for the products or create their own products and ask other to offer new features. Users also are being able to like, dislike and comment other features. This is very helpfull for product owners to know what users want more. Every feature would have a status which is being set by the owner of the product. For example statuses "Accepted" or "Rejected". This way users would know did the product owner see their offer or he just ignored that.
